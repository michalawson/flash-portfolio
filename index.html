About Me

My name is Barry Allen and I am an assistant scientist from the Criminal and Forensic Science Division of Central City Police Department. I had a reputation for being very slow, deliberate, and frequently late, which frustrated the people around me. One night, as I was preparing to leave work, a lightning bolt struck a nearby shelf in my lab and doused me with a cocktail of chemicals, which put me in a coma for 9 months. When I woke up, I was not the same man - I found that I could run extremely fast! I wanted to do more with my new ability and so I became a crimefighter known as The Flash. I store my superhero suit compressed in a special ring via the use of a special gas that could compress cloth fibers to a very small fraction of their normal size.
